AI-Generated Recipe Example

Surplus Ingredient Special: Mediterranean Chicken Bowl

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts (expiring soon)
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes (surplus)
  • 1/2 cucumber (surplus)
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese
  • 1/2 cup cooked quinoa
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Season chicken breasts with salt, pepper, and oregano. Grill for 6-7 minutes per side.
  2. While chicken cooks, dice cucumber and halve cherry tomatoes.
  3. Slice cooked chicken into strips.
  4. Assemble bowls with quinoa base, topped with chicken, tomatoes, cucumber, and crumbled feta.
  5. Drizzle with olive oil and serve immediately.
